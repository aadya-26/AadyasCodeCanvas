Project 1: Breathing Flower

This project is a 1-minute breathing exercise where a flower blooms and shrinks with your breath.

Details

This interactive project is designed to help you with mindfulness and breath awareness through a flower that grows and shrinks with your breathing.

Instructions: Follow the on-screen flower as it blooms with each inhale and shrinks with each exhale.
