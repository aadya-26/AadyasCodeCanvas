Hi!

I'm Aadya, a passionate creator exploring various forms of artistic expression through technology, design, and interactive projects. I believe in the power of art and creativity to inspire and connect people.

My Journey

My work explores themes of mindfulness, self-reflection, and playful interaction. Through my projects, I aim to create experiences that encourage curiosity and personal growth. Here's a brief overview of my artistic journey:

  • Exploring creative coding and design
  • Integrating interactive art with real-world themes
  • Continuing to experiment with new forms of digital expression

My Vision

I hope my projects will inspire others to engage with art in meaningful ways and allow them to explore their own creativity. I also want to promote the importance of mindfulness in our increasingly digital world.
